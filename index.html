Shiv Surya

I am a Research Staff Member with Prof.Venkatesh Babu's lab in Indian Institute of Science in Bangalore, India, working on machine learning and computer vision. I received my M.S in Electrical Engineering from University of Southern California in 2015. At USC, I worked in Shrikanth Narayanan's lab on liveness detection in speech. I obtained B.E degree in Electrical Engineering from RVCE in 2013. My Erdös number is 5.

Research Interest

My main research interests are in machine learning applied to computer vision and speech. In particular, I am interested in learning strategies for deep learning, generalized object detection and cross-depiction
