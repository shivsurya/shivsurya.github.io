About Me

I am a Research Staff Member with Prof.Venkatesh Babu's lab in Indian Institute of Science in Bangalore, India, working on machine learning and computer vision.

I received my M.S in Electrical Engineering from University of Southern California in 2015. At USC, My degree emphasized on Machine Learning and Signal Processing.

I obtained B.E degree in Electrical Engineering from RVCE in 2013. During my B.E, I was a computer vision research intern at ADE.

Research Interests

My primary research interests are in machine learning applied to computer vision. In particular, I am interested in generalized object detection (cross-depiction), learning strategies for deep learning, heirarchial deep networks, deep-ensembles and dense object counting.

As a Research Staff Member with Prof.Venkatesh Babu's lab in Indian Institute of Science in Bangalore, India, I work on deep Learning for computer vision.
